The “Get” Folder: Inspiring Action

This folder contains the pages that are all about action. These are the pages we want to lead people to when they are ready to make a decision and move forward.

What to create here:

  • Service Pages: Detailed pages that describe our services and their benefits.
  • Special Offer Pages: Landing pages for promotions, discounts, or special deals.
  • “Contact Us” or “Request a Quote” Pages: Pages with clear calls-to-action for getting in touch.
  • Sign-Up Pages: Pages where users can sign up for a newsletter, a trial, or an account.

The goal of these pages is to make it as easy and appealing as possible for a visitor to take the next step. The language should be clear, direct, and persuasive.

Internal Linking:

  • These pages are the final destination in the customer journey. They should be linked from relevant “Consider” hub pages.
  • Avoid linking out to “Discover” or “Consider” pages from here, as we want to keep the user focused on the action we want them to take. An exception might be a link to a case study or testimonial that supports the decision to act.
